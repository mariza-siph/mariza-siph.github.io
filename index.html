Daily Routine

I wake up at the crack of dawn (same time as my neighbour the donkey) then I demand to go on my morning walk so i can run around in the empty fields and ensure i make my everlasting ear infection worse. once that is done i go home, bark at cats to ensure the house is protected. Following these busy morning activities, depending on the weather, i take a nap either by the fire place or at my spot over the stairs where i have a birds eye view of the house. Once I'm well rested I ensure that all my humans are made aware of my existance and give me the correct amount of pets. When i get the right amount of pets, I get served my dinner, go on a liesurely walk and return home to get a full nights sleep (unless of course I sense a cat in the garden)
